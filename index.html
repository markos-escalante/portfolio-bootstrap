About Me

Hello! My name is Markos Escalante. I was born on July 6, 1992 in Panorama City, Ca. I lived in Panorama City until i was 13 and moved to Palmdale for highschool. After completing highschool at Highland High School, I decided to join the US ARMY at the age of 18. I was deployed to Guantanamo Bay where i was prison guard in Camp V. After my time in the Army, I began working in the drug and alcohol rehabilitation field as a technician! My passion to help those who want to better themselves helped me excel in this field. I am now on a path to becoming a full stack web developer and I'm excited to learn and grow!

Thanks for checking my page out!
